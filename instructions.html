Timetable Help Center

Step-by-step help for each main part of the app.

Open Main App Open Timetable View
1) Week Navigation
  • Use Prev Week and Next Week to move one week at a time.
  • Use Today to return to the current week quickly.
  • Use Now to jump directly to the current time line.
2) Views
  • Week View: standard weekly layout.
  • Day View: focus on one day, useful on small screens.
  • Full View: show all available day columns together.
3) Editing Cells
  • Tap or click a cell and type your class, task, or reminder.
  • Use short keywords like "meeting", "lunch", "project" for visual chips.
  • Use the quick add button + to add rows/events faster.
4) Customize Panel
  • Open Customize to change themes, colors, and time grid.
  • Choose interval (15/30/60 min) and workday or full-day ranges.
  • Use reset/undo tools if you want to revert changes.
5) Calendar & Holidays
  • Switch to Calendar for date-based events and birthdays.
  • Select your country to load public holidays.
  • Add events to single days or a date range.
6) Regional Time
  • The app detects your current timezone automatically.
  • The hamburger Instructions drawer shows your live local time.
  • If your device timezone changes, the app time display updates automatically.
